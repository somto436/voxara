Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 2025

Who We Are

Voxara is a Nigerian consumer research platform connecting companies with verified Nigerian participants. We are committed to protecting your personal data in line with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023.

What Data We Collect

For participants: name, email address, phone number, state of residence and occupation. For companies: contact name, work email, company name and research interests.

How We Use Your Data

  • To match participants with relevant research studies
  • To contact companies about their research requests
  • To send platform updates and study availability notifications
  • To process payments to participants upon study completion

Who We Share Data With

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with research clients. Individual participant identities are never disclosed to clients without explicit consent.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as required by Nigerian law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us.

Your Rights

Under the NDPA 2023, you have the right to access, correct or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights contact us at voxara.ng@gmail.com.

Contact

For any privacy concerns email us at voxara.ng@gmail.com.
